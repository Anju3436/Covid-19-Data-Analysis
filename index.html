CONCLUSION



This analysis provides a comprehensive overview of the global impact of COVID-19 using country-wise and region-wise data. The findings reveal that the distribution of confirmed cases,deaths,and recoveries is highly uneven,with a small number of countries contributing to the overall case count. While higher confirmed cases are often associated with higher death counts, variations in recovery and fatality rates suggest differences in healthcare infrastructure and response strategies across countries. Additionally, the analysis of WHO regions highlights geographical disparities in the spread and severity of the pandemic. Since the dataset represents a snapshot in time rather than a time-series, the study focuses on comparative insights rather than trend analysis. Overall, the use of data visualization techniques enables a clearer understanding of the pandemic's global patterns and supports informed decision-making.
